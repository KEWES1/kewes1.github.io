Privacy Policy

Welcome to the JDE Julian Date Converter (kewes1.github.io). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services. Our goal is to provide a transparent and user-friendly experience while respecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information (e.g., names, email addresses) unless you voluntarily provide it through direct communication (e.g., contacting us via GitHub). Our website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements, which may collect anonymized data such as browsing behavior or device information to serve personalized ads. For details on Google’s data practices, please see Google’s Privacy Policy.

2. Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and enable ad functionality. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand site usage and serve relevant advertisements. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of ads but will not impact the core features of our date converter tool.

3. Third-Party Services

We partner with Google AdSense to monetize our website through advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies to collect anonymized data for ad personalization. We do not control these third-party services and encourage you to review Google’s Privacy Policy for more information on their practices.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. Since we do not collect personal data through our website, there is minimal risk of data breaches. Any data handled by Google AdSense is subject to their security measures, as outlined in their privacy policy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our website, please contact us via GitHub at kewes1. We value your feedback and are happy to assist.
