JDE Julian Date Conversion Tool

One of the most useful tools for JDE users is converting Julian dates to calendar dates and vice versa. Below are Excel formulas I’ve tested and shared to simplify your JDE reporting and data analysis. Click to toggle the formulas:

JDE Julian Date to Calendar Date:
=DATE(IF(0+(LEFT(A1,1))<3,2000,1900)+LEFT(A1,3),1,RIGHT(A1,3))

Calendar Date to JDE Julian Date:
=IF(YEAR(A1)<2000,YEAR(A1)-1900&RIGHT("000"&(A1-DATE(YEAR(A1),1,0)),3),YEAR(A1)-2000&RIGHT("000"&(A1-DATE(YEAR(A1),1,0)),3))

Copy these formulas into Excel to convert dates instantly. For example, the Julian date “125365” becomes December 31, 2025, in calendar format. These are perfect for generating accurate reports or syncing data across JDE modules.
