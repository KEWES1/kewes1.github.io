Excel Formulas for JDE Julian Date Conversion

Use these formulas in Excel to convert JDE Julian dates to calendar dates and vice versa.

JDE Julian to Calendar Date (MM/DD/YYYY):

=DATE(1900+INT(A1/1000),1,1)+MOD(A1,1000)-1

Where A1 is the cell with the JDE Julian date (e.g., 123456). Format the cell as MM/DD/YYYY.

Calendar Date to JDE Julian (CYYDDD):

=(YEAR(A1)-2000+100)*1000+(A1-DATE(YEAR(A1),1,1))+1

Where A1 is the cell with the calendar date (e.g., 12/31/2023).
