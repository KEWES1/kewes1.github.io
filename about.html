About JDE Julian Date Converter

Welcome to the JDE Julian Date Converter, a free online tool created to simplify date conversions for JD Edwards EnterpriseOne users.I’m a JD Edwards expert with 25 years of experience, now living in the beautiful country of New Zealand. My passion is sharing my deep JDE knowledge to help professionals in ERP, logistics, manufacturing, and supply chain management work more efficiently.

With decades of hands-on experience in JD Edwards systems, I understand the challenges of working with JDE’s unique Julian date format (CYYDDD). This tool was born out of a desire to make date conversions seamless, allowing you to convert JDE Julian dates to standard calendar dates (MM/DD/YYYY) and vice versa with ease. Whether you’re an administrator, analyst, or consultant, this converter is designed to save you time and reduce errors in your workflows.

Based in New Zealand, I’m committed to supporting the global JDE community by providing accessible, reliable tools. The JDE Julian Date Converter is just the start—I aim to share more resources and insights to empower JDE users worldwide. Your feedback is invaluable in shaping future tools and content.

Thank you for using the JDE Julian Date Converter! If you have suggestions, questions, or want to connect, please reach out via GitHub. Let’s continue to make JDE work smarter for everyone.
